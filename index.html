How to Read

Volume I first, then Volume II. The second volume assumes the vocabulary of the first — Hooks, Legs, Flywheel, SER/BBP/EBU, the Six Archetypes, Bold Beats, above water, programmatic enough — and sharpens the edge of each term at the exact moment a bet meets its first stranger.

Each volume is self-contained. Volume I is a complete framework for choosing and funding what to build. Volume II is a complete framework for learning what it becomes once users touch it. Together they are one manual for farming the kind of luck that compounds.
